Cryptocurrencies

What are cryptocurrencies?

Cryptocurrency are a form of digital currency that is based on blockchain technology. There are thousands of unique cryptocurrencies circulating. Some of the more well known cryptocurrencies are Bitcoin and Ethereum. Cryptocurrency can be use for purchase of items and services, or invest in it like how one invest in stocks. At Coinbase, you can trade with more than 100 types of cryptocurrencies or send and receive cryptocurrencies.

What is Bitcoin?

Bitcoin is one of the most popular cryptocurrencies. It was launched in 2009, and created by Satoshi Nakomoto. Bitcoin is originally intended to be used as a form of electronic payment method based on cryptograohic proof. Similar to other cryptocurrencies, Bitcoin is also built on the blockchain network. This ensures that Bitcoin would be secure and not be faked easily. The first item bought with Bitcoin are two pizzas using 10000 Bitcoins, which are worth around USD 500 million today.
